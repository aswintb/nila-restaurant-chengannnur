About Us

Welcome to Nila

Nila, named after the legendary Bharatapuzha (River Nila), embraces the rich cultural heritage of Kerala's history. Just like the river, Nila Restaurant flows with a commitment to provide the finest culinary experience rooted in tradition, while ensuring uncompromising quality and affordability. Located in the vibrant town of Chengannur, Nila has been a culinary icon in the region since 2008.
